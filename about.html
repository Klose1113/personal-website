About Me – Full Biography

I am Tian-Yi Lu, an undergraduate in Biomechatronics Engineering at National Taiwan University, passionate about bridging mechanical systems, electronics, and intelligent software.

My journey began with curiosity about robotics during junior high school, which evolved into hands-on experience building drones, designing autonomous robots, and working with machine learning for precision agriculture.

At NTU, I’ve been actively involved in research on autonomous UAVs for greenhouse plant phenotyping, combining 3D reconstruction, computer vision, and embedded control systems to create efficient and accurate plant monitoring solutions.
