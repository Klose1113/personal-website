About Me

I’m Tian-Yi Lu, an undergraduate in Biomechatronics Engineering at National Taiwan University. I specialize in robotics, autonomous drones, and 3D reconstruction.

I’ve led multiple engineering projects — from greenhouse UAVs to robot vacuums — and enjoy building systems that connect hardware with intelligent algorithms.

I’m currently applying to master’s programs and am excited to contribute to cutting-edge research in robotics, agriculture, and AI.
