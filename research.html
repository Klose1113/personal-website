Research

This page highlights my research experiences, including my current work on UAV-based phenotyping and autonomous drone navigation.

Indoor Drone Navigation for Muskmelon Phenotyping

Investigating 3D reconstruction accuracy using various UAV flight trajectories in GPS-denied greenhouses. Techniques include RGB-D SLAM, Gaussian Splatting, and trajectory error analysis.
