About Me

I’m Tian-Yi Lu, an undergraduate student in Biomechatronics Engineering at National Taiwan University. My passion lies in building intelligent robotic systems — from UAVs that autonomously navigate greenhouses to vacuum robots that map indoor spaces.

I enjoy solving interdisciplinary problems at the intersection of hardware, software, and AI. My current research focuses on 3D reconstruction and plant phenotyping using autonomous drones.

I'm applying for master’s programs to further develop my skills in robotics and contribute to research that improves real-world systems.
